FAQ

how long is the turnaround time?
photos will be returned to the client within 4-7 days from when they are taken. please allow up to 14 days in busier times of year. rush edits can be purchased for an additional fee. video work can take up to 30 days, but is usually given within 10-20 business days.
can i reschedule?
rescheduling is free, as long as the schedule permits the day you wish to reschedule to.
what does edited photos mean?
the photos look great off of the camera, but we enhance color, remove blemishes and stray hairs, and return the photos to you ready to be posted on all social media with no extra work needed. body modification edits are a premium add on and can be purchased for an additional fee.
do you travel?
absolutely. not only do i travel, but it is my favorite thing to do. while we frequent kentucky, west virginia, virginia, tennessee, and ohio... for a fee we will travel anywhere in america.
can i bring my friend(s) to the shoot?
it is heavily encouraged to bring at least one friend to help advise, make you more comfortable, and help you however they can. i recommend 2-4 people for any outside shoots. however, large groups of people take away from shoot time. ultimately, it is up to you. bring everyone you need to feel comfortable. any shoots inside the studio are limited to 2-4 guests ASIDE from those being photographed.
where is your studio?
the studio is currently located in my home, therefore the location and address will not be publicly posted for now until we move to an outside location. for a photo tour of the studio, please message us on facebook and the address will be given upon booking.
